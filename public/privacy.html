Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to provide the LeadRadar service. This includes:

  • Authentication credentials and session tokens.
  • Search parameters used to query the underlying data providers.
  • API keys provided by the user (stored locally in your browser session or config).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your search queries and API keys are sent directly to the respective third-party APIs (such as Apify) to fetch lead data. We do not store your generated leads on our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with Apify and Google Maps. Use of our service is also subject to their respective privacy policies and terms of service. We are not responsible for the data collection practices of these third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement standard security measures to protect your sessions. However, you are responsible for keeping your API keys secure. Do not share your config files or session tokens.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact support@prompticflow.com.
